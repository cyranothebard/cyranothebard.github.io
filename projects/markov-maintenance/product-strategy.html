Product Strategy: Markov-Based Predictive Maintenance

Market analysis, competitive positioning, and go-to-market strategy for interpretable AI solutions in aviation maintenance operations.

Executive Summary: Market Opportunity

The predictive maintenance market represents a $28.2B opportunity by 2026, with aviation maintenance being a high-value segment requiring interpretable AI solutions. Our Markov-based approach addresses the critical gap between performance and explainability in safety-critical applications.

Key Market Insights
  • Market Size – $28.2B by 2026, growing at 25.2% CAGR
  • Aviation Segment – High-value applications with strict safety requirements
  • Competitive Advantage – Interpretable AI vs. black-box solutions
  • Regulatory Environment – EASA/FAA requirements for explainable AI systems
