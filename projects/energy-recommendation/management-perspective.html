Management Perspective

  • Delivery plan: phased milestones, weekly demos, risk register maintained.
  • Team: DS, MLE, and DevOps collaboration; clear ownership and interfaces.
  • Risks: data gaps, concept drift, deployment rollbacks; mitigation strategies.
  • Outcomes: reliable forecasts feeding downstream optimization and reporting.
Delivery Plan
  • Phase 1: data pipeline and baselines; Phase 2: LSTM and evaluation; Phase 3: serving and dashboards; Phase 4: pilot.
  • Weekly demos, backlog grooming, and risk reviews; clear Definition of Done per milestone.
Risks & Mitigations
  • Data Gaps: implement fallback features and imputation; SLA with data providers.
  • Concept Drift: scheduled retraining and drift alarms trigger review windows.
  • Deployment Rollbacks: blue/green or canary; versioned artifacts enable quick revert.
Stakeholders
  • Operations (primary), planning (analysis), compliance (policy), and IT (platform).
  • RACI: product owns roadmap; DS/MLE own modeling; DevOps owns reliability.
Outcomes
  • Improved forecast accuracy and operational recommendations reduce curtailment and waste.
  • Measurable KPIs: RMSE/MAPE, avoided curtailment, API uptime, user adoption.
