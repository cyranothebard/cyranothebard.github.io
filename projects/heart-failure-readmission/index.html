Heart Failure Readmission & Resource Optimization

Predict 30-day readmission risk and forecast staffing resources to improve outcomes and reduce cost.

Project Overview

We build a two-part solution: (1) readmission risk prediction for heart failure patients, and (2) downstream staffing resource forecasting to align nursing hours with patient needs. The pipeline follows a healthcare-ready approach with clear separation of data prep, modeling, and reporting.
