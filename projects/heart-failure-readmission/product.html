Product Strategy

User Personas
  • Clinician/Case Manager: triage high-risk patients; needs interpretable risk factors.
  • Nursing Supervisor: staffing plans; needs nursing-hour forecasts by care level.
  • Operations/Finance: monitor ROI; needs dashboard KPIs and trend reports.
KPIs
  • Clinical: readmission rate reduction; calibration and fairness checks.
  • Operational: staffing utilization; on-time discharges; unit load balance.
  • Reliability: API uptime; alert latency; user adoption.
Roadmap
  1. Pilot: HF unit; validate KPIs and workflow fit.
  2. Scale: multi-unit rollout; governance sign-off; training.
  3. Integrations: EHR hooks (read-only); role-based dashboards.
